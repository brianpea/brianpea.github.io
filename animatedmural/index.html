My Animated Mural (2020)

A stop-motion film that uses paper cut-outs and familiar objects to bring a part of my history to life.

This film was originally made as a teaching aid for UCLA Community School's Multigenerational Afterschool Arts (MASA) program, for participants to consider what they would memorialize if their histories lived on in an animated mural.

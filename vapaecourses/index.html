VAPAE 2023-24 Courses (2023)

A series of print and animated designs to announce the courses offered by UCLA's Visual and Performing Arts Education (VAPAE) for the 2023-24 academic year.

Inspired by ceramic tiles from the Arts and Crafts movement, the designs use naturalistic, interconnected elements to visually exemplify the program's approach to coursework.

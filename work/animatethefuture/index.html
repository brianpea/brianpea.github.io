Animate The Future! (2017)

An eight-lesson unit for first grade, based off of the various visual art skills used in an animation pipeline.

Working in "film crews" of their peers, students are asked to choose a social issue and respond to it in a claymation film. Throughout this process, students are afforded the opportunity to author a narrative of their future, while modeling the teamworking skills helpful in manifesting them into reality.

This unit was initially taught at Nora Sterry Elementary in the spring of 2017. Special thanks to Ms. Reese and the students of Room 14, as well as Jessica Bianchi, Sara Murdock, Arushi Singh, and my peers in the VAPAE Arts Ed Teaching Sequence.

Paper Mache Planters (2020)

A video lesson on making paper mache planters based on our goals.

This lesson is a part of the Multigenerational Art Project series, which focuses on using art to build communities across age gaps.

The video was produced as part of VAPAE TV and the VAPAE Program at UCLA. Video editing was done by Tigran Nersisian and Jane Kim, and lesson handout design was done by Jane Kim. Special thanks to Raymundo Baltazar and Belinda Casas.
